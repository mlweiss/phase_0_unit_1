Why Peer-Pairing in DBC?

Peer-pairing allows for a greater sharing of new ideas and provides a different resource outside of reading and working by onesself. It can also bring a more positive atmosphere to the problem solving process and helps to solidify ideas through greater communication.

Driver V Navigator

The driver-navigator analogy comes from the idea that one person is holding the map or directions and is describing where the project/vehicle should go while the other person, the driver, follows the directions and takes an active role in seeing to their implementation.
